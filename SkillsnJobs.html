Project Manager

Overview

Period: Minimum 10-week contract
Salary: $2300 p/w (equates to $120,000 p.a.), plus super
Schedule: 40 hours, Monday to Friday
Work Remotely: Yes

Team Background

404 is a small, student-based group, founded in 2021 to meet the demands of a series of university assignment tasks. The team began work on a niche, industry focused project that aimed to help users mathematically convert a temperature sensor signal into a meaningful temperature, without the use of specific equipment. Deployed as an app, there has since been investment to grow the project beyond its original scope.

The Role

The successful candidate will be responsible for managing delivery of the project, reporting directly to investors, contributing to continuous improvement, and supporting colleagues across various other roles.

Responsibilities
  • Oversee day to day running of the project, including establishing priorities, coordinating, and managing staff
  • Develop and manage project plans, schedules, budgets, and risk assessments
  • Confirm solutions are aligned with the agreed scope and contract
Key Skills/Qualifications
  • Experience in full project lifecycle ownership
  • IPM Certified Practice Project Manager (CPPM) or similar
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to meet deadlines
  • Ability to work autonomously or in team situations
Additional Beneficial Skills
  • Understanding and experience using HTML
  • Understanding and experience using JavaScript
  • Experience using Quasar framework
  • Background in metrology or calibration testing, in particular, temperature measurement
  • Knowledge of industry standards

  • Ensuring visual consistency across platforms as the project grows
Key Skills/Qualifications

  • Experience with various multimedia focused apps and the ability to learn new ones extremely fast
  • General knowledge on how to produce and edit videos that fit certain criteria
  • General creativity
  • Excellent time management
  • Ability to work in a team and autonomously
  • Ability to meet deadlines
